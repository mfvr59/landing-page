Some things I believe:

  • Enthusiasm matters.
    • You excel in what excites you.
    • Personal drive is needed for energy, and energy is needed for progress.
  • Building serendipity is your best teacher.
    • "An event characterized by an unexpected and pleasant discovery."
    • You learn what you actively look for.
  • People.
    • Be kind. Be open-minded. Be honest.
    • Treating others compassionately makes you grow.
    • Share your knowledge. Gatekeeping = Deceleration.
  • Bias for action is your best bet.
    • Advancement is hindered by fear.
    • We know less than what we think. Embracing that you're starting is the first step.
    • It's better to get dopamine from improving your idea than getting it validated.
